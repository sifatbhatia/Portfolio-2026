Parallel
Architectures

Beyond sequential logic lies the massive concurrency of the GPU. Thousands of cores working in perfect unison to render worlds and simulate intelligence.

SIMD Architecture

Single Instruction, Multiple Data. While the CPU acts as a brilliant soloist, the GPU is an entire symphony orchestra, executing the same operation across thousands of threads instantly.

Tensor Cores

Specialized silicon designed solely for matrix multiplication. The backbone of modern AI, allowing neural networks to learn and infer at speeds previously thought impossible.

Ray Tracing

Simulating the physical behavior of light in real-time. By tracing the path of light as pixels, GPUs achieve photorealism that blurs the line between simulation and reality.
